About the Plaza

A Cornerstone of
Glen Ridge Community

Ridge Plaza has served as the heart of the Glen Ridge community for years, bringing together a curated mix of businesses that make everyday life more convenient and enjoyable. From personal care and wellness to dining and professional services, our tenants are dedicated to delivering exceptional experiences.

Conveniently located at 222 Ridgewood Ave, we are easily accessible to residents throughout Glen Ridge, Bloomfield, Montclair, and the surrounding Essex County communities. Ample parking and extended evening hours make us the go-to destination any day of the week.
